# 🎬 Bible Videos: Curator's Guide

The Bible Videos module is a high-fidelity, curated gallery of functional, embeddable, and high-quality Biblical content for all ages.

## 🚀 How to Use the Gallery
1.  Navigate to **🎬 Videos** in the "More" menu.
2.  Browse your **Featured Video** (it's the large banner at the top).
3.  Scroll through the **All Videos** grid (responsively formatted for all devices).
4.  Each entry includes a high-definition thumbnail, a engaging title, and a Biblical category (e.g., Jesus, Old Testament, Kids).

## 🍿 Recommended Content
The gallery features verified content from high-quality sources, including:
*   **The BibleProject**: Excellent for youth and adults to understand the story of the Bible.
*   **Saddleback Kids**: Engaging, animated Bible stories for elementary-aged children.
*   **Topical Studies**: Specific videos on "How to Read the Bible", "The Gospel", and more.

## 📺 Watching & Sharing
*   Click **Watch Now** on any video card to open the interactive YouTube embed.
*   Videos are carefully selected to ensure "Allow Embedding" is enabled.
*   Use these videos as **lesson openers**, **Sunday school transitions**, or **mid-week devotionals** to visually engage your congregation or students.
