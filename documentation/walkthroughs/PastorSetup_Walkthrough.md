# 👨‍🏫 Pastor's Quick-Start Guide: Launching Your Church Hub

Welcome to the ultimate guide for Church Leaders! This walkthrough describes the process for a Pastor or Ministry Leader to initialize their digital congregation on BibleFunLand.

## 🏁 Step 1: The Initial Launch
1.  Log in to your BibleFunLand account.
2.  Click the **More** menu in the top navigation bar.
3.  Select **⛪ Church Hub**.
4.  If this is your first time, you will be greeted by the **Magical Church Setup** screen.

## 🏗️ Step 2: Building Your Hub
1.  **Church Name**: Enter the official name of your congregation (e.g., "Mount Zion Family Church").
2.  **Initialize**: Click the **🚀 Initialize Church Hub** button.
3.  **The Magic Happens**: Behind the scenes, BibleFunLand generates a unique, permanent **6-digit Invite Code** for your specific church and sets up your private database portal.

## 📢 Step 3: Inviting Your Congregation
1.  Once initialized, your **Church Dashboard** will load.
2.  Look for the **INVITE CODE** displayed in the gold-trimmed banner at the top of your screen.
3.  **Share the Code**: Give this code to your families, youth groups, and Sunday school teachers. 
4.  **How They Join**: Members simply go to the **🤝 Join Church** page in their own "More" menu and enter your 6-digit code.

## 📊 Step 4: Measuring Transformation
As your members join and start their Bible adventures:
*   **Leaderboard**: You will see their names, roles, and current streaks appear in real-time.
*   **Aggregate Stats**: Watch the "Active This Week" and "New Badges" meters fill up as your community grows in faith together!
*   **Pastoral Care**: Use the "Last Active" column to identify members who may need a word of encouragement or a check-in.
