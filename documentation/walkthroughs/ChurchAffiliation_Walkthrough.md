# 🏛️ Church Affiliation Program: Partner Guide

The Church Affiliation module is designed to help local churches, Christian schools, and ministries unite their mission with BibleFunLand to reach more families.

## 🏆 Partnership Tiers
We offer three levels of partnership to fit any organization size:

1.  **🤝 Kingdom Friend (Free Forever)**
    *   **Best for**: Small churches & local ministries.
    *   **Perks**: Listed in our Partner Directory, receive a digital Kingdom Resource Kit, and get a "Partner Badge" for your website.
2.  **⚡ Mission Ally**
    *   **Best for**: Growing churches & faith-based schools.
    *   **Perks**: Everything in Kingdom Friend + featured spotlight, a co-branded landing page, and events listed in our Community Calendar.
3.  **👑 Kingdom Partner**
    *   **Best for**: Businesses, publishers, and large ministries.
    *   **Perks**: Everything in Mission Ally + logo on the homepage, revenue-share opportunities, and early access to new AI tools.

## 🚀 How to Apply
1.  Navigate to **🏛️ Partner With Us** (or **Partnership**) in the "More" menu.
2.  Review the **Partnership Tiers** to see which fits your mission.
3.  Scroll to the **📬 Apply Now** section.
4.  Fill out the application form with your Organization Name, Type, and Tier interest.
5.  Click **🚀 Submit Application**.

## 📬 What Happens Next?
*   **Email Confirmation**: Your application is sent directly to our partnership team via **EmailJS**.
*   **Review Process**: We typically review and respond within **3–5 business days**.
*   **Onboarding**: Once approved, we will reach out to set up your directory listing or co-branded landing page.

## ❓ Common Questions
*   **Is it free?** Yes, the "Kingdom Friend" tier is 100% free.
*   **Can we upgrade?** Absolutely. Most churches start as Friends and upgrade to Allies as they see the engagement levels grow.
*   **What is a Co-Branded Page?** We create a dedicated URL on BibleFunLand (e.g., `biblefunland.com/partner/your-church`) that features your logo and ministry mission.
