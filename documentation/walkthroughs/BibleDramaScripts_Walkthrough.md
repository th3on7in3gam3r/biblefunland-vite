# 🎭 Bible Drama Scripts: Leader's Guide

Welcome to the AI Performance Engine for BibleFunLand! This tool allows pastors, teachers, and drama directors to generate complete, high-quality theater scripts tailored to their specific needs.

## 🚀 Getting Started
1.  Navigate to **🎭 Drama Scripts** via the "More" menu.
2.  Input the **Bible Story or Theme** (e.g., "The Prodigal Son" or "Fruit of the Spirit").
3.  Select the **Target Audience** (Preschool, Elementary, Youth, or Adult).
4.  Choose the **Number of Scenes** (Mini-skit to Full Play).
5.  Set the **Target Length** (3 to 15+ minutes).
6.  (Optional) Enter your **Cast Details** (e.g., "4 boys, 2 girls" or "Large group").
7.  Click **🎬 Generate Bible Script**.

## 📖 What's Included in Every Script?
Every generated script is a complete "Curriculum in a Box":
*   **🎭 Script Overview**: A concise summary of the play's core message.
*   **👥 Cast of Characters**: Personality descriptions and casting advice.
*   **👗 Costume & Set Suggestions**: Practical, low-budget ideas for your stage.
*   **🎬 Full Script**: Dialogue with professional stage directions and emotional cues.
*   **💡 Director's Notes**: Expert tips on pacing, blocking, and tone.
*   **🙏 Discussion Guide**: Deep-dive questions to help your congregation process the Biblical message.

## 💡 Pro-Tips for Printing
*   Once generated, click **📋 Copy All** to copy the script to your clipboard.
*   Paste it into Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or Canva to format and print for your cast!
*   Use the **💡 Director's Notes** section to plan your rehearsals before the first gathering.
