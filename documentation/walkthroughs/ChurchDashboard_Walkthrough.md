# ⛪ Church Hub & Dashboard: Congregational Guide

The Church Hub turns BibleFunLand from a personal app into a powerful congregational tool. This allows pastors and small group leaders to foster a digital faith community.

## 🚀 For Leaders (How to Set Up Your Church)
1.  Navigate to **⛪ Church Hub** in the "More" menu.
2.  Follow the **Initialize Church Hub** prompt.
3.  Choose a name (e.g., "Grace Community Church").
4.  Copy your unique **6-digit Invite Code** (provided in the gold banner).
5.  Share this code with your members! (e.g., in your church bulletin, Sunday slides, or WhatsApp group).

## 🔥 Using the Dashboard Tracking
The Church Dashboard allows you to monitor your congregation's spiritual engagement in real-time:
*   **👥 Total Members**: The number of people who have joined your hub.
*   **🔥 Active This Week**: Members who have checked in or read scripture in the last 7 days.
*   **🏆 New Badges Earned**: Total achievements unlocked by your congregation this week.
*   **⚡ Total Streaks**: The aggregate number of current daily streaks.

## 👥 Congregation Leaderboard
Scroll down to see the **Member Rankings**:
*   Identify members with high streaks (celebrate them!).
*   Reach out to members whose "Last Active" date is more than a week ago to provide care and support.
*   Monitor user roles (General, Youth, Teacher, etc.).

## 🤝 For Members (How to Join)
1.  Navigate to **🤝 Join Church** in the "More" menu.
2.  Enter the **6-digit Invite Code** shared by your leader.
3.  Click **Connect to My Church**.
4.  Your progress (streaks/badges) will now automatically contribute to your church's aggregate stats!
