# ⛪ Pastor Access Request Guide: Unlocking the Church Hub

Welcome to the definitive guide for church leaders looking to access the advanced pastoral tools on BibleFunLand! This walkthrough defines the verification process to ensure the security and integrity of our Congregational Hubs.

## 🏁 Step 1: Navigating to the Verification Portal
1. **Access Your Profile**: Log in to your BibleFunLand account and navigate to your **👤 Profile** page via the top navigation bar.
2. **Select Account Role**: Scroll down to the **Profile Info** section and click the **⛪ Pastor** role button.
3. **The Modal Appears**: This will launch the secure **Pastor Verification Modal**.

## 🛡️ Step 2: The Two Paths to Access
You will see two tabs at the top of the modal: **I have a Code** and **Request Access**.

### Path A: I have a Code (If you were independently verified)
1. Select the **I have a Code** tab.
2. Enter the unique, secure access code provided directly by the BibleFunLand admin team (e.g., `GRACE2024`).
3. Click **✅ Verify**. 
4. *Success!* Your account is immediately upgraded to the Pastor role.

### Path B: Request Access (If you are a new leader)
1. Select the **Request Access** tab.
2. **Fill out the Details**: You will be prompted to enter essential information to verify your ministry:
   - Your Full Name
   - Church Name
   - City / Location
   - Denomination (Optional)
   - Church Website (Optional)
   - Additional Notes (Optional)
3. **Submit**: Click the **📬 Submit Request** button.

## ⏳ Step 3: What Happens Next?
Once you submit your request:
1. **Immediate Confirmation**: A success screen displays in the modal, and you will instantly receive an email confirming we have received your request.
2. **Admin Review**: A notification ticket with all your provided church details is dispatched to our admin team.
3. **Approval (24–48 hours)**: Once our team verifies your church details, an administrator will reply to your email with your secure **Pastor Verification Code**.
4. **Final Step**: Return to your profile, click the Pastor role, select "I have a Code," and enter your new code to unlock the Church Hub!
